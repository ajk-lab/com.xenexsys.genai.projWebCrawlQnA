SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals
Goal 17 of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) is to strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development. This goal aims to foster collaboration among countries and stakeholders to achieve the SDGs.

Partnership Data

Key Objectives

Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development.
Enhance policy coherence and coordination among stakeholders at all levels.
Encourage and promote effective public, public-private, and civil society partnerships.
Support technology transfer and capacity-building to achieve the SDGs.

Why It Matters
Partnerships and collaboration are essential to address the complex and interconnected challenges of sustainable development. SDG 17 recognizes that no single entity or country can achieve the goals alone, and global cooperation is required.
Get Involved
If you are interested in contributing to the achievement of SDG 17, you can:

Participate in initiatives that promote collaboration and partnerships for sustainable development.
Support organizations and projects that work towards achieving the SDGs through cooperation and resource sharing.
Advocate for inclusive and effective partnerships in your community or at the national and international levels.
