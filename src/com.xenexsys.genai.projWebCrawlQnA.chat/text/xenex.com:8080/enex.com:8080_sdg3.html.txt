SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being
Goal 3 of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) is to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. This goal aims to achieve universal health coverage, reduce child and maternal mortality, combat diseases, and improve overall health.

Health Data

Key Objectives

Reduce maternal mortality and preventable deaths of newborns and children.
End the epidemics of diseases such as AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and neglected tropical diseases.
Ensure access to safe and effective healthcare services for all.
Promote mental health and well-being.

Why It Matters
Good health and well-being are essential for individuals and communities to thrive. Achieving this goal contributes to economic and social development, as well as overall happiness and life satisfaction.
Get Involved
If you are interested in contributing to the achievement of SDG 3, you can:

Support healthcare initiatives and organizations that provide medical services to those in need.
Promote awareness of health issues and preventive measures in your community.
Advocate for policies that improve healthcare accessibility and affordability.
