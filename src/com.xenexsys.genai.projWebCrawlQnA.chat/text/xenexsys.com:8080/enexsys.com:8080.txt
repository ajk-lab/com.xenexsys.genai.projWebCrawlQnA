Ajay:Welcome to the SDG Awareness Portal
This portal is dedicated to raising awareness about the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and their significance in addressing global challenges. Our mission is to inform and inspire individuals and communities to take action towards a more sustainable and equitable world.
Objectives of this Portal
Our portal aims to achieve the following objectives:

Inform and Educate: Provide information and resources related to the SDGs, including their goals, targets, and the importance of each goal.
Raise Awareness: Create awareness about critical global issues addressed by the SDGs, including zero hunger, climate action, quality education, and more.
Visualize Data: Use charts and visual representations to illustrate progress and challenges in achieving SDGs using real-world data from sources like the World Bank.
Promote Action: Encourage individuals, communities, and organizations to take action, support initiatives, and contribute to the achievement of SDGs.
