SDG 15: Life on Land
Goal 15 of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) is to protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems. This goal aims to combat land degradation, halt biodiversity loss, and ensure the sustainable management of forests.

Land Data

Key Objectives

Combat desertification and restore degraded land.
Protect and sustainably manage forests, halt and reverse land degradation and halt biodiversity loss.
Promote the conservation and sustainable use of terrestrial and inland freshwater ecosystems.
Combat poaching and trafficking of protected species.

Why It Matters
Preserving terrestrial ecosystems is crucial for biodiversity, soil health, and carbon sequestration. Protecting and restoring these ecosystems is essential for the well-being of the planet and future generations.
Get Involved
If you are interested in contributing to the achievement of SDG 15, you can:

Participate in reforestation and afforestation initiatives in your community.
Support organizations working to combat land degradation and protect endangered species.
Engage in conservation efforts to protect and sustainably manage terrestrial ecosystems and biodiversity.
